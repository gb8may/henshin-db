This app is NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Toei Company, Bandai Namco, Tsuburaya Productions, or any other official rights holders of Tokusatsu content. All trademarks and copyrights belong to their respective owners.

1. About This Application

Pocket Henshin is an independent, fan-created educational reference tool that provides:

  • Glossary: Japanese-to-English/Portuguese terminology translations for Tokusatsu fans
  • Character Database: Fan-compiled information about Tokusatsu characters
  • TokuMap: Community-sourced filming location guide
  • Community Links: Curated links to fan communities, content creators, and artists
  • Offline Access: Data caching for use without internet connection
  • Multi-language Support: Interface available in English, Portuguese, and Japanese

All content is fan-compiled for educational and entertainment purposes. This is NOT an official catalog or licensed product.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Anonymous visit statistics to understand how our app is used (page views, general usage patterns).
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for compatibility purposes.
  • Local Storage: The app uses local storage (localStorage) to cache data for offline access and remember your language preferences.

2.2. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

This app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data for personalized advertising. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage information

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

2.3. Data Stored in Supabase

Our app uses Supabase (a third-party database service) to store publicly accessible content:

  • Character information (fan-compiled)
  • Glossary entries (community translations)
  • Publication references
  • Collectibles information
  • Community links and resources
  • Anonymous visit statistics

We do NOT store personal information, user accounts, or login credentials.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app functionality
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements (via Google AdMob)
  • To enable offline functionality through local caching
  • To remember your language preferences

4. Third-Party Services

4.1. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2. Supabase

We use Supabase to host our database. Supabase's privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information or maintain user accounts, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can:

  • Clear your device's local storage to remove cached data
  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Intellectual Property Notice

All Tokusatsu-related trademarks, character names, and related content are the property of their respective owners (including but not limited to Toei Company, Bandai Namco, Tsuburaya Productions).
